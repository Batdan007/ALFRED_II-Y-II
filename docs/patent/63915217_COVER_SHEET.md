## FOR NON-PROVISIONAL CONVERSION

When converting to non-provisional, you will need:

1. **Application Data Sheet (ADS)** - Form PTO/AIA/14
2. **Specification** with detailed claims
3. **Formal Drawings** (replacing any informal sketches)
4. **Declaration/Oath** - Form PTO/AIA/01
5. **Claims** (separate section)
6. **Abstract** (150 words max)
7. **Filing Fees** (~$1,600 for small entity)
